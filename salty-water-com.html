System Parameters

  • TDS of 1000 to 5000 PPM
  • Operating Pressure 14 - 16 bar
  • Minimum feed pressure 3 Bar
  • Operating temperature 25 deg
  • Feed water turbidity less than 1 NTU
  • Feed water silt density index (SDI) 3 Maximum
  • Chlorine must be removed if present in the feed water prior the system
  • Pretreatment for water hardness using a sofener or antiscalant injection should be added
    in order to avoid scaling of the membrane

Key Features

  • Compact, heavy duty, powder coated frame
  • Proven component used throughout the system
  • Thin film composite membrane
  • Fibreglass or stainless steel membrane housing pressure vessels
  • Filter cartridge and housing
  • Automatic inlet feed soleniod valve
  • Permeate Flow Meter
  • Concentrate Flow Meter
  • High pressure stainless steel RO pump
  • Corrossion resistant materials on high pressure side
