About Us

Our Brand Story

Norwa Africa was established with a purpose to provide reliable solar and water treatment solutions in Africa. We endeavor to address the water scarcity, the challenging water quality as well as the growing demand for green energy through solar solutions and water treatment machines.
Since its establishment, Norwa Africa has been on a journey, seeking solutions in water pumping, solar power, water treatment for African communities and governments.
With the growing water scarcity, need for renewable energy and the numerous water quality concerns in Africa, Norwa Africa dedicates itself to provide reliable water treatment solutions in the most professional approach, to uplift our communities.
We are proud of it's success and look to the future with enthusiasm, to grow with the needs of our customers, to transform communities and change lives.
