Reverse osmosis salt water purication

Water was boosted by raw water pump, and filled into multi-media filter and activated carbon filter and then flow into sediment filter after pressurized by booster pump to remove substances bigger than 5um , then feed into RO system for desalination treatment after pressurized by high pressure pump. Concentrate water was discharged or recovered. The permeate water flow into the RO product tank and was sterilized by UV,then delivered to user’s point by delivery pump. After operation for a while, the equipment should be chemical cleaned.

minimum raw water pressure：40PSI（2.8KG/CM2）

water temperature：4 oC―――45 oC

turbidity： SDI < 5

Components specification for the Reverse Osmosis unit

Sand filter
Although most of the suspended impurities have been removed, a small amount of suspended solids remains in the tap water treated by municipal waterworks. Turbidity and other indicators can not meet the requirements of reverse osmosis membrane for feeding water quality. In order to make the feeding water turbidity of reverse osmosis membrane less than 1ntu, sand filter is the set in front of the system. Sand filtration is a process of water purification, which can effectively remove turbidity by passing raw water through a certain thickness of granular materials. When there are too many impurities in the filter layer, the pores in the filter layer will be blocked, the resistance of water interception will increase, and the filtration speed will be reduced. In order to recover the original filtering speed, it is necessary to wash off the impurities accumulated in the pores of the filter materials with clean water regularly Activated carbon filter
After disinfection of municipal tap water, the residual chlorine at the end of pipe network is strong oxidant. If it is not removed, it will cause irreversible damage to the resin and reverse osmosis membrane. Adsorption treatment with activated carbon can completely remove the residual chlorine. Activated carbon is strong adsorption with large capacity. The main reason is its porous structure, and its specific surface area is as high as 500-700m2 / g. Because of the developed pore structure and large specific surface area, activated carbon can completely adsorb residual chlorine and some organic matters in water, and has good removal effect on color and odor. water softener
The softener is mainly used to remove calcium and magnesium ions which are easy to scale in water, so as to prevent the chemical scaling at the concentrated side of RO membrane, especially the last piece of membrane in the RO machine and damaging the membrane element. Sediment filter and filter cartridge
Sediment filter is the last filter device before raw water entering the RO membrane. It can effectively remove the particles that are bigger than 5um to ensure the RO membrane operate safely and stably.
When the pressure difference between sediment filter inlet and outlet surpass 0.5Mpa, filter cartridge should be changed timely. Filter cartridge should be installed in right position, otherwise, it will be damaged or even lost the filtration function. Feed water solenoid valve
The function of the motorized valve is to shut off the pipeline between raw water and membrane automatically when the equipment stops in order to prevent the water from flowing into membrane and cause pollution to membrane. The specification of the motorized valve is DN32. Low pressure switch
It is a protective device to control the system operation. The value could be set according to the system technics requirement. When the pressure of the raw water supply is lower than the set value, the switch will stop the system automatically to prevent the high pressure pump from running idle when short of water or no water and causing damage. The set value for this system is 0.07Mpa. High pressure pump
This system adopts CNP pump, model CDL4-14，380V 50HZ. It's an important component of RO main machine. Its function is to supply the water source with a certain flux and pressure. It’s quality has enormous influence on the system. The pump can’t run idle or overload for long period when operation. The air inside should be removed according to requirements frequently, and the electric part should be kept dry. RO Membrane
This system adopts semi-permeable T.F.C. RO membrane, model LP21-4040, 1 - 8 pieces. RO membrane is the key part of the RO system, it decides the capacity and quality of the system Pump exit regulate valve
The main function of the shutoff valve installed after the pump is to regulate and control the flux of feed water of the RO membrane. It regulates the pressure and water volume inside the membrane when working together with the concentrate regulate valve. The valve should be opened slightly before starting the system, and open gradually when the pump begin operation to prevent the damage to the membrane due to instant high pressure. And then, the operator regulates each and every data. The specification of the regulating valve is DN25. High pressure switch
Adopt JC-230 of 3S from Korea. It is a protective device to ensure the system is operating below the maximum pressure. When the pressure of the raw water supply is higher than the set value, the switch will stop the high pressure pump automatically. It can prevent the system from high pressure damage. The set value for this system is 15kg/cm2. Pressure gauge
This system include 3pcs pressure gauge: Pressure gauge before filtration, Pressure gauge after filtration and system pressure gauge. The pressure gauge before and after filtration indicated the pressure the water flow into and flow out sediment filter. When the pressure difference reach to 0.05Mpa, the filtration cartridge need to replace. It is very important in the real operation to know the pressure difference by the two gauges. When regulating the operation parameter, the operator should regulate the pressure on the basis of the system pressure gauge, especially in the condition that the system pressure is on the upper limit and the membrane pressure difference is big. System pressure gauge shows the pressure of water before entering into the membrane.When adjusting the working condition, it need to accord to the system pressure, it should not be higher than 1.5Mpa Permeate flow meter and concentrate flow meter
The function is to calculate the permeate flow and concentrate flow. By these two meters, the operator can get the data of the permeate flow and concentrate flow, and calculate the ratio for permeate to concentrate water which helps the user to regulate the system according to the ratio and output. Concentrate regulate valve
This valve is an important part of the RO system. Its main function is to regulate the inner membrane pressure to balance the permeate and concentrate water proportion. It can regulate the inner membrane pressure and permeate output better when working together with the pump exit regulate valve. This system adopts DN32 stainless steel shut-off valve. The valve should be totally opened before the start of this system to prevent the damage due to the pressure soaring of the inner membrane when the system starts. Flushing solenoid valve
The main function is time start to lower the inner membrane pressure and expedite the water flow speed. The concentrate flow pass through in large volume in order to flush the membrane surface. This valve is an important part of the RO system. It's a key part of RO. The specification of the flushing solenoid valve for this system is DN25. Conductivity meter
The main function is to show the product water quality.when the system operation. The model of the brainpower conductivity meter is CCT3320.

Maintenance of Reverse Osmosis

Water pump
1.Before maintenance, make sure that the pump should be power-off to prevent accidental start-up.
2.The bearing and shaft seal of the pump need no maintenance.
3.If the pump will be out of service for a long time, remove a coupling protection cover before draining, and inject a few drops of silicon licon oil into the shaft between the pump head and the coupling, so as to prevent the shaft seal from being stuck during reuse.
4.Motor bearing: The motor without oil nozzle needs no maintenance. If there is a oil nozzle (including 11kw motor or above), use Esso unirex N3 or similar high temperature lithium grease. Please refer to the instructions attached on the fan cover for details. In case of seasonal operation (when the motor is stopped for more than 6 months), lubrication shall be conducted before starting the motor.
5.Anti freezing treatment: When the pump is not used in frost period, the liquid in the pump must be drained to prevent pump damage. The liquid in the pump can be discharged by water injection hole at the head and the drainage hole at the bottom. Pay attention to the safety of people or objects when draining water. When using the pump again, tighten the exhaust plug or replace the drain valve.When the drain valve needs to be replaced, the bypass valve must be loosened first, and then the water injection plug must be unscrewed. To install the drain valve, tighten the water injection plug before tightening the bypass valve.

Sand filter
1. Check the condition of the filling material every half a year or once a year. If it is found that the filling material is insufficient, the filter material shall be supplemented in time; if the filter material is seriously worn, the filter material shall be replaced.
2. Often check whether the pipeline leakage, valve opening and closing is good. The pipeline, pipe fittings and valves with problems should be repaired or replaced in time.
3. Always check whether the pressure gauge and flowmeter are accurate. If there is any error, it should be repaired or replaced in time.

Activated carbon filter
1. Change the filter material every half a year or once a year, and detect the residual chlorine at the outlet. If it exceeds 0.1mg/l, it should be replaced immediately.
2. Often check whether the pipeline leakage, valve opening and closing is good. The pipeline, pipe fittings and valves with problems should be repaired or replaced in time.
3. Always check whether the pressure gauge and flowmeter are accurate. If there is any error, it should be repaired or replaced in time.

Water softener
1. The filter material shall be replaced every half a year or once a year, and the residual hardness of outlet water shall be detected. If it exceeds 0.03mmol/l, it shall be replaced immediately.
2. Often check whether the pipeline leakage, valve opening and closing is good. The pipeline, pipe fittings and valves with problems should be repaired or replaced in time.
3. Always check whether the pressure gauge and flowmeter are accurate. If there is any error, it should be repaired or replaced in time

Reverse Osmosis membrane maintenance

1. Equipment warning or environmental requirements
1.1Avoid freezing The equipment must not be in icing condition (0 ℃). It is recommended that the minimum temperature for equipment maintenance is 5 ℃ to ensure that freezing will not occur.
1.2 Avoid high temperature High temperature should be avoided during transportation, storage and standby. It is recommended that the maximum storage temperature of the equipment under non operating conditions is 40 ℃. Avoid storing or operating the equipment in direct sunlight. Because the temperature inside the RO membrane pressure vessel may exceed the recommended maximum temperature. Note:If the temperature is higher than 45 ℃, the water permeability of the membrane will decrease; if the temperature exceeds 50 ℃, the membrane will be damaged. Attention should be paid to the selection of installation site, transportation and storage of RO equipment. 1.3 Prevent RO membrane dried To ensure that RO membrane has moisture at any time, do not open it before start up or during storage. 1.4 General conditions of storage The equipment should be stored in a low temperature and dry place and covered. Make sure that the equipment is not on the way, where it is easy to damage the equipment. For the storage and handling precautions of various chemicals used in reverse osmosis system, please refer to the chemical treatment section of this manual. 1.5 The oxidant must be removed from the raw water If there are similar oxidants (such as: subbromic acid, hydrogen peroxide, ozone, potassium permanganate, sodium persulfate, chlorine) will cause irreversible damage to the reverse osmosis membrane. 1.6 Other chemicals The raw water and cleaning fluid entering the system shall not contain any components (such as oil, etc.) that are difficult to be mixed with water. The use of these components will cause irreversible blockage to reverse osmosis membrane. Do not use other chemicals not specifically listed in this manual. The use of incompatible chemicals will cause irreversible damage to the reverse osmosis membrane and pump components
2. Normal life spam of membrane
The normal life spam of RO membrane is 1-3 years (determined by the water quality and water consumption), and the influence of the service life may reduce the production water quantity and the production water quality. After using for a period of time, it is necessary to replace the new membrane and repair the system performance.
3. RO membrane blocking
The RO system may be blocked after several months of operation, which will reduce the production water volume or quality. After a period of time, it is necessary to clean the membrane and repair the system performance
4. Membrane protection measures
The basic protective measures of RO membrane are described as follows
4.1The turbulent velocity of salt channel is the key factor to keep the membrane surface clean.
4.2 Obstructions, TSS and colloids in raw water should be treated.
4.3The concentrate is kept below the salt path limit to prevent the film from scaling.
4.4PH value is the best range to prevent the system from scaling.
4.5Avoid physical damage to membrane and equipment, and pay attention to the blockage of concentrated water pressure during restart.

Summary
After a period of normal operation, the surface of the membrane in the system is deposited by some substances (mineral precipitation, biological substances, sediment, etc.), and the interior of the membrane is dark and humid, it is easily reproduction for bacteria and microorganisms.
There are three related questions to the successful maintenance the membranes: why maintain membranes? What time is suitable for operation and maintenance of osmosis membrane? How to maintain membrane? It will be described as follows.

Why maintain membranes?
When RO system running, bacteria in the water are removed, and salt substances harmful to human body are removed as much as possible. In this way, pollutants are blocked on the membrane surface, and accumulated sludge and mineralized sediment (scaly shape) can cause membrane surface blockage, thus reducing membrane permeability and desalination rate. The blockage of biological substances, scales or sludge is deepening, and the blocking of membrane module is also increasing, which leads to the decrease of flow on the membrane surface. At the same time, the reduction of membrane surface flow leads to the gradual blockage of the flow path in the membrane module. Once the channel in the membrane module is blocked, it may be recovered by chemical cleaning. If it cannot be recovered, the membrane module needs to be replaced.
What time is suitable for operation and maintenance of osmosis membrane?
The membrane module needs to be maintained when there are the following signals and conditions:
a) RO system has been shut down for more than 5 days.
b) There are a lot of bacteria in the product water quality analysis.
c) The desalination rate is 50% higher than that after the first start-up or the last cleaning operation (e.g. 10% to 15%).
d) The pressure is 20% higher than that of the first start-up or the last cleaning (for example, the raw water pressure of 300 psi minus the concentrated water pressure of 250 psi equals to 50 psi, and the pressure ratio of 50 psi to 60 psi needs maintenance. Note: psi value is using a pressure indicator)
e) When the production flow is reduced by 10% than which of the first start-up or the last time cleaning. (such as the production flow rate is reduced from 28gpm to 25gpm), it will also be caused if the production flow rate remains unchanged and the raw water pressure increases by 10

How to maintain membrane?
Membrane maintenance is accomplished by chemical cleaning, chemical disinfection or a combination of the two. Usually, the combination of chemical cleaning can achieve good results.
Note:The design and maintenance of the system is based on the permeate in the membrane module shell and system circulation pipe.
Cleaning methods is usually depend on contaminants or obstructions.
The three influential parameters in cleaning are as follows:
1. Temperature
2. Speed
3. Time
The best cleaning temperature is about 45 ℃, which means that the heating and cooling depends on the environmental conditions. Furthermore, a certain flow rate needs to be distributed to the membrane module to dissolve the insoluble blockage. Finally, it is necessary to operate the CIP pump circularly in order to loosen the blockage on the membrane during soaking. Note:For safe operation of chemical cleaning, refer to relevant acid and alkali regulation data and relevant regulations provided by manufacturers
Under the following conditions, the composite membrane should be properly treated and stored in the reverse osmosis pressure vessel
a) Short term preservation of RO membrane
Generally, the storage time is 1 to 5 days (determined by the reproductive activity of bacteria)
Use pure water for normal shutdown flushing and venting
Re rinse every 5 days (max 30 days)
Washing with 1% sodium bisulfite solution can reduce the possibility of biological contamination
b) Long term preservation of RO membrane
Usually refers to more than 30 days of preservation
Cleaning reverse osmosis membrane module
Wash and store with suitable fungicides (0.15% isothiazoline, 1% sodium bisulfite or 0.1% - 1.0% formaldehyde)
If the temperature is less than 27 ℃, use fungicide every 30 days to rinse and store
If the temperature is higher than 28 ℃, use fungicide every 15 days to rinse and store
