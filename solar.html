Solar Modules

Polycrystaline Panels

Solar modules are particular designed for solar power systems generating eco-friendly and reliable energy from the sun, which provides an ideal solution for various applications where no grid connection is available.

Contact us for more information →

Key Features

  • Available from 200 watts to 340 watts
  • Strong anodized aluminum frames to strengthen the load and wind resistance.
  • High transparency low iron tempered glass with stiffness and impact resistance.
  • Anti-ageing low, EVA and excellent anti-climate back sheet
  • Reliable bypass diodes to prevent overheating (hot spot effect) and to minimise power loss by shading
  • Certified quality management (ISO 9001) guarantee best quality

Applications

  • Large on/off-grid solar power station
  • Solar pumping systems
  • Residential roof-top systems
  • Rural electrication
  • Commercial/industry building roof-top system
