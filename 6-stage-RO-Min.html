6 stage Reverse Osmosis systems
with Mineralizer

Purify your water with the addition of mineral stones

This machine uses Reverse Osmosis to purify water. Reverse Osmosis is a treatment process for the removal of all dissolved solids and bacteria to provide totally pure water for domestic and small-scale application.With addition of mineral stones which contains various minerals such as potassium,sodium and other micro elements which are essential to good health.

Basic Features

  • Water Flow : 50 - 100 Gallon per Day
  • Power : 100~240V, 50/60HZ, any power plug
  • Pressure tank : 3.0g plastic tank

Product Features

  • Small size and Easy operation
  • The single filter can either:
    • Sediment filter to remove dirt, rust other solid objects or,
    • Carbon filter that takes out 98% of the chlorine and organic chemicals, herbicides, pesticides or,
    • Block activated carbon a fine sediment reduction for removal of odor, taste, cyst sand turbidity.
    • Membrane(NSF certificated) which uses Reverse Osmosis is a treatment process for the removal of all dissolved solids and bacteria to provide totally pure water for domestic and small-scale application.
    • Post carbon filter,to adjust the taste and odor
    • Mineralizer - this andesite contains nourishing minerals for good health
  • Energy saving, environment protection
